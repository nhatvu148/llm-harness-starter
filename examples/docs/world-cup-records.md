# FIFA World Cup — Records and Milestones

## Most titles

- **Brazil — 5** (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002)
- **Germany — 4** (1954, 1974, 1990, 2014)
- **Italy — 4** (1934, 1938, 1982, 2006)
- **Argentina — 3** (1978, 1986, 2022)
- **Uruguay — 2** (1930, 1950) and **France — 2** (1998, 2018)
- **England** (1966) and **Spain** (2010) have each won once.

## Players

- **Miroslav Klose** (Germany) is the all-time top scorer with **16 goals**
  across four tournaments (2002–2014).
- **Just Fontaine** (France) holds the record for most goals in a single
  tournament: **13 goals in 1958**.
- **Pelé** (Brazil) is the only player to win **three** World Cups (1958, 1962,
  1970).

## Teams

- **Brazil** is the only nation to have appeared at **every** World Cup.
- No team has successfully defended the title since Brazil won back-to-back in
  1958 and 1962.
