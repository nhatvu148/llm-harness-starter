# Erling Haaland — Player Profile

Erling Braut Haaland is a Norwegian striker, widely regarded as one of the most
prolific goalscorers in modern football.

- Born **21 July 2000** in Leeds, England (his father, Alf-Inge Håland, was
  playing in England at the time). He represents **Norway** internationally.
- Position: **striker**, known for pace, physicality, and finishing.

## Clubs

- Came through **Bryne** and **Molde** in Norway, then **Red Bull Salzburg**,
  then **Borussia Dortmund** (2020–2022), before joining **Manchester City** in
  **2022**.
- Scored a **hat-trick on his UEFA Champions League debut** for Salzburg
  against Genk in September 2019.

## Manchester City — 2022–23 (treble season)

- Scored **36 Premier League goals**, a record for a single 38-match Premier
  League season (surpassing the previous mark of 34).
- Scored **52 goals in all competitions** that season.
- Won the **Premier League Golden Boot** and the **PFA Players' Player of the
  Year** award.
- Manchester City won the **treble**: the Premier League, the FA Cup, and the
  UEFA Champions League.

## World Cup note

- Despite his club goalscoring, Haaland has **never appeared at a FIFA World
  Cup** — Norway did not qualify during his career so far. That is why he does
  not appear in the World Cup records in this dataset.
