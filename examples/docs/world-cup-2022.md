# FIFA World Cup 2022 (Qatar)

- **Champion: Argentina** — their third title and first since 1986.
- The **final** against France finished **3–3** after extra time; Argentina won
  **4–2 on penalties**.
- **Lionel Messi** won the **Golden Ball** (best player of the tournament) and
  captained the winning side.
- **Kylian Mbappé** (France) won the **Golden Boot** as top scorer with **8
  goals**, including a **hat-trick in the final** — the first hat-trick in a
  World Cup final since 1966.
- **Emiliano Martínez** (Argentina) won the **Golden Glove** as best
  goalkeeper.
- The final was played at **Lusail Stadium**, which has a capacity of about
  **88,966**.
