# FIFA World Cup 2026 (USA, Canada, Mexico)

The 2026 World Cup is co-hosted by the **United States, Canada, and Mexico** —
the first World Cup with three host nations and the first to feature **48
teams**.

## Format (confirmed)

- **48 teams** in **12 groups of 4**.
- **104 matches** in total (up from 64 in previous editions).
- The top two from each group, plus the **8 best third-placed teams**, advance
  to a new **Round of 32** knockout stage.
- Scheduled dates: **11 June – 19 July 2026**.
- The **final** is scheduled for **MetLife Stadium** (East Rutherford, New
  Jersey, USA).

## Results (as of 14 July 2026)

The tournament has reached the **semifinals**:

- **France vs Spain** — semifinal at AT&T Stadium, Dallas (14 July 2026).
- **Argentina vs England** — semifinal at Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Atlanta
  (15 July 2026).
- **Argentina** enter the semifinals as the **reigning champions** (2022
  winners).
- No champion has been crowned yet — the **final is 19 July 2026** at MetLife
  Stadium.
