# Recent FIFA World Cups (2006–2026)

A summary of the last six tournaments. See the dedicated files for more detail
on 2022 and 2026.

## 2006 — Germany

- **Winner: Italy** (4th title). Runner-up: **France**.
- Final: **1–1** after extra time; Italy won **5–3 on penalties**.
- Notable: **Zinedine Zidane** was sent off for headbutting Marco Materazzi in
  the final — the last match of his career.
- Golden Boot: **Miroslav Klose** (5 goals).

## 2010 — South Africa

- **Winner: Spain** (1st title). Runner-up: **Netherlands**.
- Final: **1–0**, an **Andrés Iniesta** goal in extra time.
- First World Cup hosted in **Africa**.
- Golden Ball: **Diego Forlán**. Golden Boot: **Thomas Müller** (5 goals).

## 2014 — Brazil

- **Winner: Germany** (4th title). Runner-up: **Argentina**.
- Final: **1–0**, a **Mario Götze** goal in extra time.
- Notable: Germany beat hosts **Brazil 7–1** in the semifinal.
- Golden Boot: **James Rodríguez** (6 goals).

## 2018 — Russia

- **Winner: France** (2nd title). Runner-up: **Croatia**.
- Final: **4–2**.
- Golden Boot: **Harry Kane** (6 goals). Golden Ball: **Luka Modrić**.

## 2022 — Qatar

- **Winner: Argentina** (3rd title). Runner-up: **France**.
- Final: **3–3** after extra time; Argentina won **4–2 on penalties**.
- See `world-cup-2022.md` for full detail (Messi, Mbappé, etc.).

## 2026 — USA, Canada & Mexico

- First **48-team** World Cup; co-hosted by three nations.
- In progress as of 14 July 2026 (semifinal stage). See `world-cup-2026.md`.
